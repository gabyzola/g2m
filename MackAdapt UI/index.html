What is MackAdapt?

MackAdapt is an adaptive learning tool built for Merrimack students and professors. Our mission is to personalize quiz experiences based on your progress — no more one-size-fits-all testing.

  • Real-time adaptation based on your answers
  • Aligned with your learning objectives
  • Easy-to-use dashboard for students and instructors

How It Works

1. Sign In

Log in to your student dashboard with your school credentials.

2. Take Adaptive Quizzes

Questions adapt to your performance in real time.

3. Track Your Progress

See what you’ve mastered and where to focus next.
